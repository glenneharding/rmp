The Future of RMP

These are the long-term goals planned for the RMP project once the artwork is complete.

1. Reprints and Other Products

Once the mural is completed, the first step will be to create high-quality reprints and derivative products. These could include posters, canvas prints, books, and merchandise to help share the message of the RMP with a broader audience while also supporting its ongoing mission.

2. The RMP Mobile App

A dedicated app will offer an interactive way for people to engage with the mural digitally. It could include detailed zoom-in features, narration, behind-the-scenes insights, and educational content to enhance accessibility and reach.

3. The RMP Video and Book Reading

Plans include producing a narrated video that walks through the mural and its themes, along with a published book that captures the full story. These formats will make the content available in both visual and literary forms, perfect for sharing in schools, churches, or events.

4. Ultimate Goal – The Mural

The ultimate vision is to have the mural installed in a large, permanent space where people can experience it in full scale. This would be the culmination of years of work—bringing the RMP to life as a monumental public work that tells its story in a powerful, immersive way.
